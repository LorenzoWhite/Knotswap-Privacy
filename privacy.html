Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Knot is a community-owned barter app. We collect only what we need to make trading work. We don't sell your data, run ads, or share your information with anyone except the services that keep the app running.

Knot has no investors and no advertisers. Your data exists to help you trade things, nothing else.

What we collect

When you use Knot we collect:

  • Account information – your email address and display name when you sign up
  • Listings – photos, titles, and descriptions of items you post for trade
  • Location – your approximate location, used only to show you nearby items and show others how far away your listings are. We never store a precise GPS history.
  • Trade activity – likes, matches, and messages between trading partners
  • App usage – which items you like or pass on, used to personalize your feed over time

How we use it

  • To show you items near you that match your interests
  • To connect you with people who want what you have
  • To send you trade match notifications and account emails
  • To improve the item recommendation algorithm over time
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs

The recommendation algorithm

Knot learns from what you like and pass on to surface items you're more likely to want. This is similar to how a social feed works. The more you use the app, the more relevant your browse experience becomes. We use your in-app behavior only. We do not purchase external data or profile you outside of Knot.

Who we share data with

We use a small number of third-party services to run the app:

  • Supabase – database, authentication, and file storage
  • Resend – transactional email (login links, notifications)
  • Apple / Google – if you use Sign in with Apple or Google, their respective privacy policies apply to that authentication step

We do not sell your data to anyone. Ever. We do not share your data with advertisers. We never will.

Location

Knot requests access to your location to show you items nearby and display approximate distance on listings. We do not log a history of your precise location. You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings — the app will still work, but distance-based features won't be available.

Your data, your choice

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Deletion removes your account, listings, trade history, and messages from our systems within 30 days.

You can also contact us directly at hi@knotswap.app to request a copy of your data or ask any questions.

Age requirement

Knot is only available to users 18 and older. Because the app involves meeting strangers to exchange items in person, we require all users to be at least 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy we'll notify you via the app or email before the changes take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests — reach us at hi@knotswap.app
