How it works

Post what you have

A book, a bike, a few hours of guitar lessons. Snap a photo, write what you are hoping to trade it for.

Browse what is nearby

Swipe through listings in your area. Like the ones that catch your eye, pass on the rest.

Match and meet

When you both like each other's things, that is a match. Chat in the app, meet up, make the trade.

Our promise

How it stays free

KnotSwap is free because the community keeps it that way. Ads, subscriptions, hidden costs. None of that here.

Community funded

Pitch in whatever feels right. A dollar, five, or nothing at all. Real people keeping a real thing alive.

Total transparency

Every invoice, every server bill, every charge is public. You see exactly where the money goes.

Honest stakes

Miss the funding goal for a year and the app goes dark. Every cost is public so you know what it takes to keep it running.

What makes KnotSwap different

Most apps want your attention so they can sell it. KnotSwap just wants to help you trade.

Barter only, always

KnotSwap is for trading things and skills directly. Payments, markup, shipping fees don't exists here.

No ads, no tracking

We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. The recommendation feed only uses what you like and pass on inside the app. KnotSwap stays free because the community funds it.

Community-funded

KnotSwap is a non-profit project funded by annual donations from the people who use it. If we do not hit our fund goal, the app goes dark until we do.

Trades near or far

Set your trade radius from a few blocks to anywhere in the country. Meet face to face when it is close, ship when it is not.
